SpaConcierge – Privacy Policy for Messaging

Last updated:

1. Information we collect

  • Phone number – provided by you when booking or calling a participating spa.
  • Message content – limited to service-related details such as confirmations, reminders, and reschedules.
  • Message metadata – delivery status, timestamps, carrier information.
  • Minimal appointment context – date/time of a booking. We instruct spas not to include treatment details or PHI.

2. How we use your information

We use this information solely to deliver service-related text messages on behalf of participating spas and to comply with carrier/legal requirements.

3. Sharing of information

We do not sell your information. We share it only with service providers needed to deliver texts (e.g., Twilio for SMS, Netlify for hosting, Google Sheets for logging). These providers process data on our behalf.

4. Data retention

We keep message logs for a limited period (generally up to 18 months) for operational purposes, unless longer retention is required by law or disputes.

5. Security

We apply reasonable safeguards appropriate for lightweight appointment messaging. No system is 100% secure.

6. No PHI

We are not a HIPAA-covered entity. Do not use this Program to send or expect sensitive treatment details. If such information is sent inadvertently, we aim to delete it upon discovery or request where feasible.

7. Your rights

You may opt out of receiving texts at any time by replying STOP. You may also request deletion of your number from active systems by emailing matt@getspaconcierge.com.

8. Children

The Program is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 where applicable). We do not knowingly collect their data.

9. Do Not Track

Our website may use cookies/analytics. We do not respond to Do Not Track browser signals.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy. New versions will be posted with a new date.

11. Contact

Email: matt@getspaconcierge.com
