Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how SpaConcierge (“we,” “us”) collects and uses personal information when you visit our website, communicate with us, or use our messaging features. We are a lightweight customer-care tool for spas and do not intend to process protected health information (PHI).

1. Information we collect

  • Business contact data: spa owner/manager name, email, phone, billing details.
  • End-customer messaging data: phone number, message content you send, timestamps, delivery status, basic device/carrier information.
  • Appointment context (optional): service-neutral details (e.g., date/time). We instruct customers not to include treatment specifics or PHI.
  • Website data: cookies/analytics (pages viewed, device/browser) and form inputs.

2. How we use information

  • Provide the Service (missed-call follow-ups, confirmations, reminders, reschedules).
  • Operate, secure, and improve the Service (debugging, analytics, abuse prevention).
  • Comply with legal obligations and carrier/A2P requirements.

3. Legal bases (EEA/UK)

We process data based on contract (to provide the Service), legitimate interests (customer support/anti-abuse), and consent (end-user opt-in to receive messages).

4. No PHI

We are not a covered entity or business associate under HIPAA. Do not use the Service to send or store PHI. If PHI is sent inadvertently, we will delete it upon discovery or request where feasible.

5. Sharing & processors

We use vetted providers who process data on our behalf:

  • Twilio (telecom/messaging delivery)
  • Netlify (hosting/build/deployment)
  • Google Workspace / Google Sheets (business operations/storage)
  • Calendly (scheduling links)
  • Analytics/cookie tools (site usage)

We do not sell personal information.

6. International transfers

Data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our providers operate. Appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs) are used where required.

7. Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above. Messaging logs are typically retained for a short operational period (e.g., up to 18 months) unless law or disputes require longer. You may request deletion where feasible.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate for a lightweight customer-care tool. No system can be 100% secure.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your data, and to object or restrict certain processing. To exercise rights, email matt@getspaconcierge.com.

10. Cookies

We may use cookies/analytics to understand site usage and improve the Service. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

11. Do Not Track

We do not respond to DNT signals.

12. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 where applicable). We do not knowingly collect their data.

13. Changes

We may update this Policy and will post the new date above.
