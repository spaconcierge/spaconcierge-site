Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers on setup, coverage, handoffs, consent, reporting, and more.

All the details
What happens after a missed call? +
We text the caller within ~60 seconds, introduce your clinic, and help them book or get answers. If needed, we escalate to your team—no loops.
Which calendars or booking systems work with this? +
We support link-based booking flows and can mirror availability from common med-spa platforms. Where direct APIs aren't available, we confirm final times with your team to avoid double-booking.
Do we have to change our number? +
No. You keep your number. We add conditional forwarding for "no answer / busy / after hours," so callers always see your existing number.
Is there after-hours coverage? +
Yes—24/7. Overnight activity is summarized for your team each morning.
How does human handoff work? +
If a question is clinical, unusual, or high-value, we flag it and route to your team (SMS/email). You can set rules for when to escalate.
Can we control tone, pricing, and policy answers? +
Yes. We load your brand voice, current pricing, promotions, cancellation/deposit policies, pre-/post-care notes, and FAQs. You can update these anytime.
What about consent and opt-outs? +
We respect standard opt-out keywords (e.g., "STOP"). We provide short consent language for your voicemail and intake forms; use where required by your local regulations.
HIPAA and privacy? +
We use HIPAA-aware workflows and secure storage practices. We avoid PHI in text; sensitive items are routed to secure channels or staff review.
How do you handle spam or wrong numbers? +
We filter obvious spam and throttle suspicious patterns. You'll see a transparent log of conversations for review.
Can it support multiple locations or numbers? +
Yes. We route by location, source number, or keywords (e.g., "Downtown"). Reporting can be split per location, too.
Languages supported? +
English by default; Spanish on request. Additional languages upon review of volume and content needs.
Deposits and cancellation policies? +
We can send your deposit link, collect confirmations acknowledging your policy, and tag the booking with the confirmation.
What reporting do we get? +
Weekly summary: missed calls recovered, new leads captured, bookings confirmed, average response time, and reminder impact on no-shows.
Trial and cancellation? +
Start with a short pilot. No long contracts—cancel anytime before the next cycle.
