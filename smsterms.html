These Terms govern your participation in the SpaConcierge messaging program (the “Program”). By providing your mobile number to a participating spa or clinic, you agree to receive service-related text messages as described below.

1. Program purpose

SpaConcierge helps spas and clinics connect with their clients through service-related SMS. Messages may include:

  • Missed-call follow-ups
  • Appointment confirmations
  • Appointment reminders
  • Reschedule options
  • Basic customer-care replies

Messages are one-to-one and are not marketing blasts. We do not use your number for unrelated promotions.

2. How to opt in

You opt in by giving your phone number to a participating spa when you call, book an appointment online, or fill out an intake form. By doing so, you consent to receive service-related texts powered by SpaConcierge.

3. Message frequency & fees

Typical frequency is 1–3 messages per appointment. Message and data rates may apply, depending on your carrier and plan.

4. Opt-out & customer care

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. You will receive a confirmation that you have unsubscribed. After that, you will no longer receive messages unless you opt in again. For help, reply HELP or contact matt@getspaconcierge.com.

5. No sensitive health information

Messages are designed to be service-neutral and must not include protected health information (PHI) or treatment details (e.g., “Botox at 3 PM”). If you see a message with such details, please notify your spa immediately.

6. Program eligibility

You must be at least 13 years old (or 16 where required by law) to participate. If you are under 18, you should have consent from a parent or guardian.

7. Privacy
